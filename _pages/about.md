I am a PhD researcher in [Computational Media and Arts](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/) at **HKUST (Guangzhou)**, where I work at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, serious game design, and cultural heritage technology. My research explores how interactive and immersive experiences — grounded in VR, LLM-driven dialogue, and embodied interaction — can deepen engagement with culture, history, and learning.

Before joining HKUST(GZ), I completed an MSc in **Serious Games and Virtual Reality** at the Glasgow School of Art (2023) and a Bachelor's in **Digital Media Art** at Tianjin Academy of Fine Arts (2022).

## Research Interests

- Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
- Serious Game Design & Learning Technology
- Cultural Heritage & Museum Technology
- LLM-driven Interactive Narrative
- Immersive Media & Virtual Reality
